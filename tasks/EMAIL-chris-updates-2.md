Chris,

I built out the things you mentioned. Here's what's new in the portal — log in and you'll see all of it.

**1. Invoices now show whether they've been paid.**
When Strand cuts a check, they can mark the invoice paid with the check number and date. GMs see the status the moment Strand updates it — they don't need to email anyone to ask "did that get paid?" anymore. The check number and date sit right next to the invoice for their records. Try it: open any invoice on the Dashboard, look at the right side — that's where the "Mark paid" button lives.

**2. GMs now have their own property page.**
Instead of just an upload screen, each GM signs in and lands on "My Property" — it shows their hotel's info up top (address, phone numbers, room count), a quick stat for unpaid invoices, the upload form, and every document they've ever sent to Strand with a paid/unpaid pill next to each one. They click any document to see the file, your note about it, and the payment status. It's their dashboard for their hotel.

To see what they see: go to **Properties**, click any hotel, then click **Preview as GM** in the top right. You'll be looking at exactly what their GM sees.

**3. Financial statements have a home.**
There's a new **Financials** tab in the menu. You and Strand can upload P&Ls, balance sheets, cash flow, budgets — pick the property, the month/year, drop the file, done. The GM at that property automatically sees it on their property page. So does Strand, and so do you. No more emailing P&Ls around.

To upload one: **Financials → Upload a statement** on the right.
